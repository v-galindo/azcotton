The Consequences of Non-Compliance may include:

THIS LAW IS NEW:

$100 per acre civil penalty to the producer on each acre that is not in compliance at tillage deadline.
(A.R.S. § 3-1086)
  

THIS PART OF THE LAW REMAINS THE SAME:

Forfeiture of plower rebate on entire FSA number.
(A.R.S. § 3-1086)
 
THIS LAW HAS BEEN MODIFIED:


 Producer responsible for cost of abatement, and may be assessed 150% of such abatement costs.

(A.R.S. § 3-1086), (A.R.S. § 3-204.01), (A.R.S. § 3-205)
