The Arizona Cotton Research and Protection Council engages in various activities designed to promote Arizona cotton.

Generally, in accordance with statute, the Council meets quarterly. They may meet more often, should the need arise. The agenda is posted at least 24 hours in advance of the meeting. On occasion, we may post some of the power point presentations from the Council meetings.
