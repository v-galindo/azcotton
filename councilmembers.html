3-1082. Cotton research and protection council; membership; terms; vacancy; compensation

The Cotton Research and Protection Council is established and is composed of nine active cotton producers to be appointed by the governor. The members shall be appointed as follows:

 1. Two each who are residents of:

(a) Maricopa county 

(b) Pinal county

(c) The Yuma, La Paz and Mohave counties area 

   2. One each who is a resident of: 

(a) Pima county  

(b) Cochise county 

(c) The Graham and Greenlee counties area
